Everything you need, built to last

Every new deck includes a brand new, long-lasting subframe, premium boards, the full installation and a tidy clean up. The $275/m² rate applies to a 20m² minimum (approx $5,500 ex. GST). For smaller decks or trickier sites the price can vary, and we'll confirm your exact quote on the day.

What can change your price

The starting price covers a standard build

Every backyard is different. Here's what we look at on site that can shift the final number.

01

Deck size

How big the deck is. A larger footprint means more subframe, more boards and more time on site.

02

Height and clearance

Whether it sits low to the ground, up high, or fully suspended. Low clearance and raised decks take more work.

03

Site access

How easy it is to get materials and tools to the spot. Tight access means more of the job is done by hand.

04

Board choice

The board range and finish you pick from the 40+ designs. Some options cost a little more than others.
